Roblox Account Verification

Link your Roblox account to your Discord account in a few simple steps.

1
Join the Discord server and run the /linktoroblox command.
2
Click the personal verification link sent to you by the bot.
3
You'll be taken to Roblox to confirm the link. Click Continue.
4
Done — your accounts are linked and your role will be assigned automatically.
