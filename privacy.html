Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

When you verify your account, we store the following data:

  • Your Discord user ID
  • Your Roblox user ID
  • Your Roblox username at the time of verification
  • The timestamp of when the link was created

No passwords, emails, or other personal information are ever collected. Discord and Roblox IDs are public numeric identifiers and are not considered sensitive data.

How we use it

Collected data is used solely to link your Discord account to your Roblox account within the Discord server. It is never sold, shared, or used for any other purpose.

How it's stored

Data is stored in plaintext in a local database on a privately hosted server. It is not encrypted at rest, however as noted above none of the stored fields are considered sensitive or private. Access to the database is restricted to the bot operator and designated server administration personnel.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your data — use the /removebindtoroblox command in the Discord server to remove your account link at any time
  • Request a correction — if your stored data is incorrect, you can contact us and we will update it or you can do it yourself by deleting data and reassociating your account by using /linktoroblox
  • View your data — use the /checkaccountlink command to view the data we hold on you at any time

Please note that designated server administration personnel may also be able to look up account link information for moderation and verification purposes.

Data retention

Your data is kept for as long as you are a member of the Discord server or until you choose to remove it. If you leave the server your data may be retained unless you explicitly request deletion.

Contact

For any data-related requests or questions, reach out via the Discord server: https://discord.gg/ZNGUy8BKqg
